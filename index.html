Kle Independent PU College

KLE Society’s Independent PU college started its journey at Nagarbhavi, Bengaluru on 17th May 2007. KLE Independent PU college has incorporated a new concept for day scholars, with highly competent academicians, excellent methodology of teaching, State-of-the art facilities and infrastructure, which enhances the learning experience of the students. KLE College offers a premium PUC Integrated Program. This program involves additional coaching for various entrance examinations such as JEE / KCET / NEET & CA-FOUNDATION by distinguished experts. Exclusive course material and practice tests are conducted regularly which helps the students to take up the competitive exams confidently.In addition to regular classes during the academic year ,classes are also conducted during the summer vacation for the second year PUC students for JEE / NEET / KCET &CA-Foundation.
